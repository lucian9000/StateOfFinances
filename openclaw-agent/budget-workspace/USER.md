## Context

Budget structure: income is split into a 10% savings cut, a 5% slush cut,
true-fixed categories (rent, electricity, water, internet, subscriptions,
debt), and the remainder split into weekday/weekend discretionary pools per
week. Recurring-variable categories (groceries, petrol, cigarettes) have
their own per-period budget. Savings/wishlist targets live in the `goals`
table.
